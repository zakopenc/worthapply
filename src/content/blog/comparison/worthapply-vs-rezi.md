## Quick Answer

**Rezi** is a fast AI resume builder focused on creating ATS-optimized resumes from scratch, while **WorthApply** is a complete job application platform with job fit analysis, resume tailoring, and application tracking. Choose Rezi ($29/mo) if you need to build a resume quickly. Choose WorthApply ($29/mo) if you already have a resume and need to tailor it for multiple jobs plus track applications.

## WorthApply vs Rezi: Different Tools for Different Stages

These tools serve overlapping but distinct purposes:

- **Rezi**: "Build an ATS-friendly resume fast" (resume creation focus)

**Simple rule**: If you're starting from zero, use Rezi to build your master resume. If you already have a resume and are actively applying, use WorthApply to optimize each application.

## Key Differences Explained

### 1. Job Fit Analysis (WorthApply Only)

**WorthApply's Unique Feature:**
- Paste job URL → AI analyzes your resume vs requirements
- Get match percentage (0-100%)
- See exactly which qualifications you have/lack
- Decide if job is worth applying to before investing time

**Rezi doesn't have this.** You create a great resume, but you still have to manually decide which jobs to apply to. This means you might waste hours applying to jobs you're underqualified for.

**Impact**:
The fit-first approach lets you cut the application volume dramatically and redirect that time toward the roles you actually have a real shot at. Instead of sending 100 identical applications and hoping, you send 10–15 carefully tailored ones to roles where the fit analysis already confirmed you clear the hard requirements.

**Winner**: WorthApply (unique value prop)

### 2. Resume Building vs Tailoring

**Rezi's Strength - Building from Scratch:**
- Choose from 8 ATS-optimized templates
- AI suggests bullet points based on your job title
- Section-by-section guidance
- Best for creating your first "master resume"
- Fast: build a complete resume in 15-20 minutes

**WorthApply's Strength - Tailoring Existing Resumes:**
- Already have a resume? Paste job URL → AI rewrites sections to match (60 seconds)
- Customizes bullet points for each application
- Adds relevant keywords naturally
- Best for applying to multiple jobs efficiently

**The Workflow Difference:**
- **Rezi**: Build once → Apply to many jobs (with manual edits)
- **WorthApply**: Have master resume → Auto-tailor for each job (seconds per application)

**Winner**: Depends on your starting point
- **Rezi** if you don't have a resume yet
- **WorthApply** if you have a resume and are applying to multiple jobs

### 3. AI Quality & Approach

**Rezi's AI:**
- Custom-trained on millions of successful resumes
- Suggests job-specific bullet points
- You select suggestions and edit
- More structured, guided process

**WorthApply's AI:**
- Uses GPT-4 (state-of-the-art language model)
- Rewrites your content to match job requirements
- More autonomous (less manual selection)
- Preserves your voice and accomplishments

**Example Comparison:**

**Original Bullet:**
> "Managed social media accounts and increased engagement"

**Rezi Suggestion (you choose):**
> - "Managed 5 social media channels reaching 50K followers"
> - "Increased social media engagement by 35% through content strategy"

**WorthApply Auto-Tailor (for Social Media Manager role):**
> "Developed and executed data-driven social media strategy across 5 platforms, increasing audience engagement by 35% and growing follower base to 50K through targeted content campaigns"

**Winner**: Tie (both effective, different styles)

### 4. Application Tracking

**WorthApply:**
- ✅ Built-in application tracker
- Track status, dates, contacts, notes
- See all applications in one place
- Set follow-up reminders

**Rezi:**
- ❌ No tracker (need separate tool/spreadsheet)

**Winner**: WorthApply (Rezi doesn't have this feature)

### 5. ATS Optimization

Both tools check ATS compatibility, but with different approaches:

**Rezi's ATS Checker:**
- Real-time scoring as you build
- Shows formatting issues instantly
- Template-based (all templates are ATS-optimized)
- Section-by-section guidance

**WorthApply's ATS Checker:**
- Upload existing resume → Get analysis
- Free unlimited scans
- Shows what ATS will extract
- Gives specific fixes

**Winner**: Tie
- **Rezi** prevents ATS issues during creation
- **WorthApply** diagnoses existing resume problems

### 6. Templates & Design

**Rezi:**
- ✅ 8+ professional templates (all ATS-optimized)
- ✅ Modern, clean designs
- ✅ One-click template switching
- ✅ Customizable colors/fonts

**WorthApply:**
- ⚠️ Exports to standard .docx format
- Focus is on content tailoring, not design
- You can apply your own template after export

**Winner**: Rezi (much better for visual resume design)
