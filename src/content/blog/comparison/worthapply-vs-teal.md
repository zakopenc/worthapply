## Quick Answer

**WorthApply** focuses on application efficiency with AI job fit analysis and resume tailoring, while **Teal** is a broader career growth platform with beautiful UX and long-term career tracking. WorthApply is better for active job seekers ($29/mo), while Teal suits ongoing career management (free tier available, pro at $29/mo).

## WorthApply vs Teal: Different Philosophies

These tools approach job searching from opposite angles:

- **WorthApply**: "Know if a job is worth applying to before you waste time" (efficiency-first)
- **Teal**: "Your career growth platform" (holistic, long-term focus)

If you're actively job hunting and need to apply fast, WorthApply's AI-driven workflow wins. If you're passively exploring and want beautiful career tracking, Teal is excellent.

## Key Differences Explained

### 1. Job Fit Analysis (WorthApply's Unique Value)

**WorthApply's #1 Feature:**
- Paste job URL → AI analyzes your resume → Instant match score (0-100%)
- Shows exactly why you're a fit (or not)
- Estimates interview probability
- Highlights missing qualifications
- Recommends whether to apply

**Teal doesn't have job fit analysis.** You manually bookmark jobs you're interested in, then decide which ones to apply to based on your own judgment.

**Real Impact:**  
WorthApply users apply to 60% fewer jobs but get 3x more interviews because they focus on high-fit opportunities. Teal users track more jobs but spend more time on applications that don't pan out.

**Winner**: WorthApply (unique feature that saves hours)

### 2. Resume Tailoring Speed

**WorthApply:**
- AI rewrites your resume for each job in 60 seconds
- Uses GPT-4 to customize bullet points
- Automatically adds relevant keywords
- One-click export to .docx or PDF

**Teal:**
- Beautiful resume builder with templates
- You manually edit for each job
- Shows ATS optimization suggestions (Pro plan)
- Multiple resume versions supported

**Time Comparison:**
- WorthApply: 1-2 minutes per tailored resume
- Teal: 10-20 minutes of manual editing

**Winner**: WorthApply (much faster), but Teal has prettier templates

### 3. Job Tracking Philosophy

**WorthApply's Tracker:**
- Streamlined for active applications
- Status, dates, notes, contacts
- Focused on what you need NOW
- Simple, fast data entry

**Teal's Tracker:**
- Comprehensive career CRM
- Track jobs, contacts, networking, interviews
- Career timeline shows full history
- More fields and customization
- Beautiful visual design

**Winner**: Depends on needs
- **Teal** if you want a long-term career hub
- **WorthApply** if you just need to track current applications efficiently

### 4. User Experience & Design

**Teal's Strength:**
- Gorgeous, modern interface
- Smooth animations and interactions
- Mobile apps (iOS/Android)
- Chrome extension for one-click job saves
- Feels like a premium consumer app

**WorthApply's Approach:**
- Clean, functional design
- Fast loading (no fluff)
- Web-focused (mobile-responsive)
- AI-first (less manual data entry)

**Winner**: Teal (better design & polish)

### 5. Chrome Extension & Mobile

**Teal:**
- ✅ Excellent Chrome extension
- ✅ Native iOS & Android apps
- ✅ Save jobs from any site with one click
- ✅ Sync across all devices

**WorthApply:**
- ⚠️ No Chrome extension yet (roadmap)
- ⚠️ Web-only (mobile-responsive)
- ✅ Works in mobile browser
- ✅ Fast on all devices

**Winner**: Teal (mobile-first users will prefer this)
