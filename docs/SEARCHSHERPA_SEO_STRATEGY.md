## 🤖 GEO / AEO OPTIMIZATION STRATEGY

### What is GEO/AEO?
- **GEO:** Generative Engine Optimization (Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Claude)
- **AEO:** Answer Engine Optimization (Perplexity, You.com, direct answers)

### Why it matters for WorthApply:
Your positioning is PERFECT for AI citations. Queries like "how do I know if a job is worth applying to" will trigger AI Overviews, and you want to be the cited source.

### GEO/AEO Checklist for Every Page:

**1. Direct Answer Block (Top of Page)**
```markdown
## Quick Answer

[2-3 sentence direct answer that AI can extract cleanly]

Example: "A job is worth applying to if your skills match 70%+ of the requirements, 
the company culture aligns with your values, and the compensation meets your needs. 
Use WorthAply's Job Fit Analyzer to get an instant match percentage."
```

**2. Question-Format H2s**
Instead of: "Job Fit Analysis"
Use: "How Do You Analyze Job Fit?"

**3. FAQ Schema (Bottom of Page)**
Add 5-8 common questions with concise answers.

**4. Comparison Tables**
AI engines LOVE extractable tables. Every comparison page needs one.

**5. Clear Entity Definitions**
Define terms explicitly:
- "WorthApply is a job application platform..."
- "Job fit analysis is the process of..."
- "ATS (Applicant Tracking System) is software that..."

**6. Credible, Specific Claims**
Instead of: "The best resume tool"
Use: "WorthApply's resume tailor uses GPT-4 to customize resumes in under 60 seconds"

**7. Supporting Evidence**
Add data points, examples, or methodology:
- "Based on analyzing 10,000+ job postings..."
- "Our algorithm matches on 150+ data points..."
- "Users see a 3x increase in interview rates..."

**8. Scannable Format**
- Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences max)
- Bullet lists
- Bold key phrases
- Clear section breaks

### AI Citation Strategy:

**Target queries for AI Overviews:**
1. "how to know if a job is worth applying to"
2. "what is a good job match percentage"
3. "how to tailor resume for specific job"
4. "should i apply to this job"
5. "best way to track job applications"
6. "worthapply vs jobscan"
7. "free ats resume checker"
8. "how to beat ats systems"

**How to win citations:**
- Create definitive guide pages
- Use clear "According to [source]" language
- Add publish/update dates
- Build topical authority (multiple pages on topic)
- Get backlinks from trusted sites
- Be cited on Reddit, Quora, industry forums

**AI-Friendly Content Format:**
```markdown
# [Question as Title]

## Quick Answer
[2-3 sentence extractable answer]

## What You Need to Know
[Bullet points of key facts]

## Step-by-Step Guide
1. [Clear step]
2. [Clear step]
3. [Clear step]

## Example
[Real-world example]

## Tools That Help
- **WorthApply** - [Brief positioning]
```
