## 🚨 CRITICAL: KEEP FINANCES SEPARATE

**DO:**
- ✅ All business income → Business bank account
- ✅ All business expenses → Business credit card/account
- ✅ Pay yourself "distributions" (transfer to personal)
- ✅ Keep receipts for everything

**DON'T:**
- ❌ Never mix personal and business money
- ❌ Never pay personal bills from business account
- ❌ Never pay business bills from personal account

**Why:** "Piercing the corporate veil" - courts can ignore LLC if you mix finances.

## 💡 QUICK ANSWERS

**Q: Can I wait until I'm profitable?**  
A: ❌ No. Liability starts with first customer. Form before launch.

**Q: Delaware or Wyoming instead of my state?**  
A: ❌ Not needed. Form in your home state (simpler, cheaper).

**Q: What if I get sued before forming LLC?**  
A: 💸 You pay from personal assets. Retroactive protection doesn't exist.

**Q: Do I need a lawyer?**  
A: 🟡 Not for simple single-member LLC. Use online service ($39-200).

**Q: Will this affect my taxes?**  
A: 🟢 Same as now (Schedule C). You can elect S-Corp later if profitable.

**Q: What if I want to add a co-founder later?**  
A: ✅ Easy. Update operating agreement, issue membership units.

**Q: Can I DIY this?**  
A: ✅ Yes! State website, $50-500, 1-2 hours. But service is easier.
