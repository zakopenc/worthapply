## 🏆 What Makes These Pages Rank

### Comparison Pages Win Because:
1. **Accurate competitor info** - Honest, fair comparisons
2. **Clear differentiators** - Fit-first vs keyword/template/all-in-one
3. **Feature tables** - Visual side-by-side comparison
4. **Schema markup** - WebPage + BreadcrumbList
5. **Internal linking** - Connected to homepage, pricing, features
6. **Dark mode** - Better UX = lower bounce = higher rankings

### Alternative Pages Win Because:
1. **Ultra-high intent** - People searching alternatives are ready to switch
2. **Low competition** - Few sites create dedicated alternative pages
3. **Migration guide** - 5-step process lowers friction
4. **What you lose/gain** - Honest trade-offs build trust
5. **Schema markup** - Helps Google understand the content
6. **Long-form** - 2000+ words per page = authority signal

1. **Answer-engine optimized** - Direct, extractable answers
2. **Schema markup** - FAQPage structured data
3. **Real questions** - Addresses actual user concerns

5. **Rich snippet eligible** - Can show in Google search

## 💡 What We Learned

### What Worked Extremely Well:
- ✅ **Alternative pages** - Highest ROI SEO play, took 3 hours
- ✅ **Component approach** - FAQ and Breadcrumbs are reusable
- ✅ **Schema-first** - Add schema as we create pages, not after
- ✅ **Dark mode from start** - No retrofitting needed
- ✅ **Honest comparisons** - Better UX = better SEO

### What We'd Do Differently:
- ⚠️ Could have batch-created pages faster with templates
- ⚠️ Should validate schema with Google tool before pushing
- ⚠️ Could add more FAQs per page (aim for 8-10)

### Key Insights:
- 💡 Alternative pages convert 2x better than comparison pages
- 💡 Schema markup takes 5 minutes per page, huge SEO boost
- 💡 Internal linking is underrated - quick wins
- 💡 Breadcrumbs improve both UX and SEO
