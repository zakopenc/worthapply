# Storyboard: "Fit First vs. Bulk Apply"
## Duration: 40 seconds
## Style: Kinetic Blackboard (Professional/Technical)

### 0-5s: The Problem (Hook)
- Visual: Split screen. Left side: "Bulk Apply" (red, cluttered, spinning wheel of death). Right side: "Fit First" (green, clean).
- Audio: Fast, punchy sound effect. "Stop spraying and praying."

### 5-15s: The Cost of Bulk
- Visual: Kinetic typography showing "500+ apps" -> "0 interviews". A stack of paper shrinking into a trash can.
- Text: "Bulk applying isn't a strategy, it's a vanity metric."

### 15-25s: The Shift (The "Fit First" Promise)
- Visual: Transition to "WorthApply" interface. Clean, focused.
- Text: "Fit analysis > Keyword stuffing." "Quality signals over volume."

### 25-35s: The Evidence
- Visual: An animated "Fit Score" increasing based on role alignment.
- Text: "Tailor for the role, not the ATS."

### 35-40s: Call to Action (CTA)
- Visual: Logo + "Stop applying, start getting hired."
- Audio: "WorthApply. Analyze your fit. Get the interview."
